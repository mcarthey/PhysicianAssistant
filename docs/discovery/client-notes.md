### Key Themes from Transcript

**Primary Goal — Physician AI Assistant (Night/Weekend Triage System)**

Martin's core vision is an AI assistant that operates as an after-hours triage system for pediatricians' patients. The problem he is solving: physicians cannot be available 24/7, but parents need guidance at night, weekends, and holidays. Other pharmaceutical companies buy physician loyalty through congress trips and gifts — Martin cannot and does not want to compete that way. Instead he wants to give physicians a valuable IT tool that keeps them closer to their patients.

**How the system should work (as described):**
1. Physician turns the system ON when going off-shift
2. Parents contact via WhatsApp (text or voice)
3. AI captures symptoms, name, child details
4. AI cross-references with medical evidence (Martin specifically mentioned **OpenEvidence** — used by Cleveland Clinic and Johns Hopkins)
5. AI provides calm, measured feedback to parents — triages urgency
6. If emergency: directs to emergency room immediately
7. If non-emergency: reassures parent, confirms doctor will follow up
8. All captured data queued for physician review when back on shift
9. Physician reviews, calls back patient, closes the loop
10. System learns over time from physician feedback

**Key design constraints Martin stated:**
- He does NOT want to capture or track the patient for his own commercial purposes — only to help the physician
- System must comply with Peruvian government health data policies
- AI must NOT make medical decisions — it assists and captures only
- Physicians must be able to turn the system on and off
- Both text and voice channels needed ("crazy moments — send voice")

**Secondary Goal — Infanzia Product Chatbot**
- Simpler chatbot for claims and surveys related to the Infanzia product line
- Martin acknowledged this is "more easy" and suggested running both in parallel
- This is the faster, lower-risk starting point

**Additional Notes:**
- Martin wants to diagram the full physician system workflow and send via email in Spanish
- He is open to a phased approach
- Multilingual capability resonated strongly — he understood the ElevenLabs voice/language pitch immediately
- He expressed interest in the AI being trainable — Mark explained the RAG/knowledge base concept and the need for a proper admin interface rather than text-based updates
