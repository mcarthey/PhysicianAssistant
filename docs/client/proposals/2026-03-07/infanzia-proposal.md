## Understanding Your Goals

From our conversation, two distinct needs emerged:

**For parents of Infanzia patients:** A reliable, knowledgeable resource available around the clock — answering questions about Biomilk formulations, Infabiotix dosing, Infavit supplements, and general Infanzia product guidance. Accurate, warm, and always appropriate in tone.

## Proposed Systems

### System 1 — Infanzia Product Assistant

A WhatsApp-based AI assistant that represents the Infanzia product line professionally and accurately. Parents can ask questions via text or voice at any hour and receive reliable answers grounded in the actual product documentation you provide.

**What it does:**
- Answers questions about product ingredients, formulations, dosing, and certifications
- Handles inquiries about where to purchase and what distinguishes each product
- Collects structured feedback and satisfaction responses
- Routes questions it cannot answer to a human representative
- Maintains a consistent, professional voice — in Spanish first, with additional language support available

**What it never does:**
- Offer medical advice of any kind
- Diagnose or recommend treatment
- Make claims beyond what the product documentation supports

**How you manage it:** A simple web interface allows you to upload updated product documents, review conversation logs, and adjust the assistant's knowledge as your product line evolves. No technical expertise required.

**Timeline:** 4–6 weeks from project start

---

### System 2 — Physician After-Hours Triage Assistant

This is the system you described in our meeting — an AI that operates on your behalf when you are off shift, so that parents who reach out via WhatsApp receive a thoughtful, calm response rather than silence.

The system listens. It captures. It assesses urgency. It reassures. And when you return, it presents everything you need to follow up intelligently.

**How it works:**

When you activate off-hours mode, parents who contact you via WhatsApp are greeted by the assistant in Spanish. The conversation proceeds naturally — the assistant asks about the child's name, age, and what the parent is observing. It collects everything clearly and structures it for your review.

If symptoms suggest an emergency — difficulty breathing, seizures, loss of consciousness, or other critical indicators — the assistant immediately directs the parent to emergency services. No hesitation, no additional questions.

For non-emergency situations, the assistant reassures the parent, confirms that you will review their information and follow up, and closes the conversation. Everything is waiting for you when you're back on shift.

**What you see when you return:** A prioritized queue of overnight conversations, each with a structured summary — child name and age, symptoms described, urgency assessment, and the parent's contact details. You can read the full transcript, take action, and mark each case as handled.

**The clinical boundary:** The assistant does not diagnose. It does not prescribe. It does not make clinical recommendations. It captures what parents share and keeps them calm while they wait for you. Every interaction states clearly that a physician — you — will review the information and make all clinical decisions.

**Your control:** The system is on when you turn it on. Off when you don't need it. You decide which patients and which contact numbers it responds to. You manage the knowledge it draws from — pediatric guidelines, clinical protocols, and any specific guidance you want it to reference.

**Timeline:** 12–16 weeks from discovery phase completion (see below)

## Data Privacy & Compliance

Patient data is sensitive. This system will be built with that as a first principle, not an afterthought.

All health information captured through the triage system will be handled in accordance with Peru's Ley 29733 (Ley de Protección de Datos Personales). This means explicit consent captured at first parent interaction, secure encrypted storage, defined data retention policies, and a complete audit trail of every interaction.

Before launch, we recommend review by a Peruvian legal advisor to confirm compliance posture. This legal review is a client responsibility — Learned Geek LLC will provide all technical documentation that review requires.
